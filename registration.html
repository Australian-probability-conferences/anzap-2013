Registration costs (includes day meals and refreshments):

  • AMSI Members: $90
  • Non-AMSI Members: $120
  • AMSI Students: Free
  • Speakers from outside of Austrlia (not currently working in Australia): Free
The costs for the workshop dinner is $65 per person. These costs apply to all participants and companions who wish to attend the dinner (optional). Kids eat free.
To make the payment, please fill out this form and either FAX it to +61 7 33653328, or send it by post to the address mentioned on the form.
This event is sponsored by the Australian Mathematical Sciences Institute (AMSI). AMSI allocates a travel allowance annually to each of its members. Students or early career researchers from AMSI member institutions without access to a suitable research grant or other source of funding may apply for subsidy of travel and accommodation out of the departmental travel allowance.
